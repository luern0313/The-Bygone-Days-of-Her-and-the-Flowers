<p>This game is a fan-made visual novel based on the popular game Genshin Impact, using its world-building and character settings.</p>

- Original script and case design, featuring captivating reasoning and suspenseful storylines
- Through investigation, deduction, and trial, peel back the layers of mystery shrouding the case.
- 7 to 8 hours of immersive gameplay
- Custom character art and variations, with nearly 30 story CGs
- Full voice acting for all characters and main storylines
- Unlockable side-game "Turtle Soup" mini-games as the main story progresses
